Biography | CV (pdf)

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department at Florida State University. I have earned my undergraduate degree in Computational Mathematics from Lomonosov Moscow State University, Russia in 2015. Prior to starting my Ph.D. studies, I have spent a couple of experient years at Rock Flow Dynamics as a C++ Software Engineer in a team responsible for the MPI aspect of tNavigator, a fast, accurate and feature-rich oil and gas reservoir simulator; my undergraduate thesis being a part of that experience. During my initial Ph.D. years, I had a fruitful collaboration with Dr Michael Gubanov on polystore databases, search and data integration, resulting in a few publications; one in ICDE, a flagship international conference in Data Engineering. Currently I'm pursuing my research interests in deep learning applied to natural language processing working with Dr Xiuwen Liu.
